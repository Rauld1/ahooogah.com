Having fun with balloons, face painting, games, and magic in San Diego since 2003!

Thanks for visiting the AhOOOgah the clown website. AhOOOgah is a long-time local entertainer available for birthday parties, festivals, grand openings or any other occasion you would like to have clown fun at!

AhOOOgah specializes in driving your kids crazy. With gentle humor, he gets the party going via silliness and jokes while twisting balloon sculptures, painting faces, doing a magic show or leading games.

If you have any questions, please feel free to send a message or if would like to book AhOOOgah for one of your events, please fill out a small booking inquiry.
